Akela Bhai (born 1 January 1998) is an Indian web designer and personal branding consultant based in India. He is the founder of akelabhai.com, a digital practice focused on web design, digital identity development, and brand strategy for individuals and businesses. He is widely recognized online under the handle @akelajiofficial.

Since beginning his career in 2020, Akela Bhai has worked with solo entrepreneurs, content creators, and small businesses across India, helping them establish a credible and visible online presence. He is the author of Brand Yourself: Build Your Identity, Grow Your Influence, and Dominate Social Media (2026), published on Amazon KDP and Google Play Books.

Early life and background [edit]

Akela Bhai was born on 1 January 1998 in India. He developed an early interest in technology and visual communication, which led him toward web design and digital tools during the formative stages of his career. His curiosity about how information is presented and consumed online eventually evolved into a professional practice focused on building digital identities for individuals and small businesses.

His career has been shaped by the rapid growth of India's digital economy and increasing demand for professional online services among freelancers, entrepreneurs, and businesses transitioning to digital-first models.

Career [edit]

Akela Bhai launched his professional career in 2020, building websites and digital content for clients seeking to improve their online visibility. He gradually broadened his scope from technical web design to the wider field of personal branding, recognizing that a website alone does not fully define how a person or business is perceived in the digital space.

Operating through his brand akelabhai.com, he has consulted with a wide range of clients — from solo entrepreneurs and content creators to small-to-medium businesses — guiding them through defining their digital identity, building functional websites, and maintaining consistent brand presence across platforms.

Web design

As a freelance web designer based in India, Akela Bhai focuses on creating clean, functional, and visually coherent websites tailored to the brand identity of each client. His design approach prioritizes usability, mobile responsiveness, and consistent visual language across all touchpoints.

Personal branding

A significant part of his consultancy involves helping professionals and businesses define their digital identity online. This includes brand language development, visual guidelines, and content strategies that maintain a coherent and recognizable presence across digital channels. His clients have spanned sectors including entrepreneurship, content creation, and professional services.

Content and publishing

Akela Bhai publishes content regularly on platforms including Medium, DEV.to, Quora, and Pratilipi, writing on topics including web design, personal branding, digital strategy, and entrepreneurship. His 2026 book, Brand Yourself, is available on Amazon KDP and Google Play Books.

Philosophy [edit]

Akela Bhai's core professional philosophy holds that digital visibility is not incidental to success — it is a foundational requirement. He argues that no matter the quality of one's work, professional impact is limited without the ability to be found, recognized, and trusted online by the right audience.

He advocates that personal branding is about communicating genuine value clearly — not self-promotion. His consulting approach emphasizes practical, actionable steps through which individuals and businesses can improve their perceived credibility and digital discoverability over time.

Online presence [edit]

Akela Bhai is active across multiple digital platforms under the handle @akelajiofficial, including LinkedIn, Instagram, Twitter (X), and Facebook. His primary digital hub is akelabhai.com, which serves as a portfolio, services platform, and publishing destination.

Through consistent cross-platform presence, Akela Bhai demonstrates in practice the same principles of digital visibility and personal brand consistency that he advocates in his professional consulting work.
